Laws of Conduct
1. After 2 consecutive red trades, trader must stop for the day.
2. Trader must stop after reaching reasonable daily goal and must not look for more opportunities.
3. Trader must aim to trade as little as possible to avoid red trades.
4. Trader must never enter into a trade with feelings of hope.
5. Trader must stick with one base strategy and shall never use other strategies.
6. Trader must always follow said rules.
